Giants

Two giants? Goddesses?

Burning, shining, radiating.

A collection of prisms.

One million tons impossibly suspended.

So light, so much light.

I, with less water, less grace,

Less of a dancer.

Will I ever get to move with you?

You said you didn't know how.

I just pretend I'm a cloud.
