vows

Love brought you to me.

(Urukundo rwarakunzaniye.)

I could never let you go.

(Sinshobora kukureka.)

You are a song too beautiful to sing.

(Uri indirimbo nziza cyane itabashwa kuririmbwa.)

You are the moon that lights the darkness.

(Uri ukwezi kumurika mu mwijima.)

You are the first and last one I want to see each day.

(Ni wowe wambere n'uwanyuma nshaka kubona buri munsi.)

You are the queen of my heart.

(Uri umwami w'umutima wanjye.)

I will always love you.

(Nzahora ngukunda)
