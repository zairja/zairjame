lime and avocado

My little lime

Mi limonito

I'll be the hand to gently squeeze out the sour

You are the zest that enlivens every hour

Your juice on my lips is like heaven

I'll never mistake you for a lemon


My little avocado

Mi aguacatita

I'll be the lime that keeps you ever green

with vitality, not envy

Never too mushy, always ripe

Making guacamole every night
